# 5Ducks - AI-Powered B2B Lead Generation

5Ducks is a B2B prospecting platform that transforms simple queries into comprehensive prospect lists with verified contacts, personalized outreach campaigns, and automated email campaign management.

## Features

- **Smart Lead Discovery**: Find companies and contacts matching your ideal customer profile
- **Verified Emails**: Get validated email addresses for your prospects
- **AI-Powered Outreach**: Generate personalized email campaigns with AI assistance
- **Campaign Management**: Schedule and track your email campaigns with intelligent pacing
