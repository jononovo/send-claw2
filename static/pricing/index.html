Frequently Asked Questions

What are credits and how do they work?

Credits are used to power your searches and contact discovery. Each search typically uses 5-10 credits depending on the complexity. Credits reset monthly with your subscription.

Can I change my plan anytime?

Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Your next billing cycle will immediately reflect the changes or cancellation.

What happens if I exceed my credit limit?

If you run out of credits, you can either upgrade your plan or wait until your next billing cycle. We'll never charge you without permission.

Is there a free trial?

Our free plan gives you 180 credits per month forever - no trial needed! You can upgrade anytime to get more credits and advanced features.
